What is AI Creativity?

An AI or (Artificial Intelligence) is a type of nerual network which simulates human intelligence through machines. AI's can learn, solve problems and make desicions based off data patterns. But how does AI manage to iterate if all it can use is existing data?

Defining Creativity

What is creativity? In the frame of AI, creativity is a systems ability to create outputs that are

  1. novel
  2. valuable
  3. and surprising

These are three qualities many psychologists and philosophers agree on. If you look on the surface, AI has all three of those qualities, but having superficial good qualities doesn't make up for a lack in substance. AI achieves its ideas through a thing called Associative Creativity; a process which involves identifying patterns across huge datasets and linking content in those datasets in ways that make it seem original, but in reality, it doesn't imaagine, and it doesnt wonder. While on the surface it seems like a creative idea, underneath, its all just math.

Levels of Creativity in AI

To fairly evaluate AI's creativity, we need to let go creativity as a concept of have or don't have. Instead, we should consider it as a spectrum with varying degrees of creative expression.

The first level of creativity is combinational creativity, the basic idea of adding well known ideas together to get a fusion. An example could be using AI to merge two differents cultures foods into one fusion, and this stuff is what AI excels at, connecting the dots.

The second level is explatory creativity, which involves pushing boundries of an already existing framework. This level of creativity is about exploring the limits of what is already known and pushing those boundaries to discover new possibilities. However some notes are: (a). the AI is still bound by existing knowedge and can still only connect the dots (b). AI must be trained on this datawhich is time and labor intensive. An example would be drug discovery and/or materials science, which AI does faster then humans most of the time with a very good accuracy rate.

The last and highest level is called transformational creativity. This is a theoretical, there is no evidence that ANY AI has this. This level of creativity involves not only seeing the patterns, but also rejecting them, and calling them wrong if it feels like it.

Whats missing?

Currently AI is doing well in the combination creativity level, but in the explatory creativity level it seems to lack. So whats missing? Its not the ability to reject, as seen in level three; but 3 other core things. AI right now is limited because of the data humans feed to it, but if it could have its own experiences, if it could feel what it was like to be a human, it might be able to achieve a higher level of creativity. In that catagory there is also failure. Without faling there is only limited learning. Humans can learn from their mistakes, AI's just forget them. The last thing is intention. AI has no intention right now. It has no desire to communicate or share. No goal its working towards to fuel it.

Conclusion

AI has come so far, it produces results we thought we could only see in science fiction, but creativity has never been about the output, its about those three elements which form a drive, a desire to create. AI is missing those, and without them, it will never be truly creative. Until AI can want, or need something, it will stay a mirror of human creativity, not a source of it. The dots are connected. And we still can decide which dots matter.
